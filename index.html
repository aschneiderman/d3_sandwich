About D3 Sandwich

D3 Sandwich is an experiment: can we make D3.JS easier to learn by teaching it using interactive, annotated examples of cool things you can do with D3?

D3 Sandwich is a project of Data Chefs, a coalition dedicated to creating a more diverse, inclusive Data Science and empowering the community by making Data Science more accessible.
